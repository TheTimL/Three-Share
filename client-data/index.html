Welcome to Three Share!

Online whiteboards are now a critical part of learning institutions and businesses. They offergreater efficiency and convenience in learning, foster engagement and drive performance.

Our product not only has the online whiteboard or canvas feature, it also has a code share systemand online chat room.

Apart from convenience and flexibility in education and business, the useof our product increases team engagement and can increase productivity.

To collaborate on a drawing in real time with someone, just send them its URL.

The anonymous board is accessible to everyone. It is a happily disorganized mess where you can meet with strangers and draw together. Everything there is ephemeral. Go to the anonymous board
